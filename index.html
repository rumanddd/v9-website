The Concept

A kitchen built around the coal grill. A bar built around conviction.

V9 runs on live fire — everything from the vegetables to the cocktail garnish passes near the coals before it reaches the table. The menu is short on purpose and changes with what the grill wants to do that week.

No tasting menus, no pretense. Just a room that gets louder as the night goes on, a bar that takes its pours seriously, and a kitchen that stays open later than it probably should.
